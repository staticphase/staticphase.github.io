About Static Phase

Static Phase is a Perth-based alternative rock trio known for their innovative sound blending rock, jazz, and experimental elements. The band formed in 2020 and quickly gained a reputation for electrifying live performances and boundary-pushing music.

Their unique sound comes from the dynamic mix of Daryl Dandyhil's textured guitar work, Cameron Hostyle’s powerful drumming, and Liam Keltryeko’s soulful vocals and saxophone melodies.

Drawing inspiration from a variety of genres and artists, Static Phase crafts songs that are as emotionally powerful as they are musically complex. The band is constantly evolving, always seeking to push their creative limits.
