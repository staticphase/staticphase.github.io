Static Phase

Perth-based Alt Rock Trio

Static Phase is a Perth-based trio pushing sonic boundaries with their raw energy and genre-blending sound. Featuring guitarist Daryl Dandyhil, drummer Cameron Hostyle, and vocalist/saxophonist Liam Keltryeko, the band delivers a dynamic mix of rock, jazz, and alternative vibes.

Daryl Dandyhil

Guitarist and tone wizard, Daryl brings melodic riffs and textured layers that define Static Phase’s unique sound.

Cameron Hostyle

The rhythmic backbone, Cameron's drumming blends precision and raw power to drive the band’s live energy.

Liam Keltryeko

Lead vocals and saxophone – Liam’s dual talents bring both grit and smoothness, making every performance unforgettable.
